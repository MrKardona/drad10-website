# PRODUCT.md — DRA.D10 Medicina Estética

## Product Purpose
Premium medical aesthetics clinic website. Converts first-time visitors into booked appointments. The design IS the brand signal — it must communicate luxury, trust, and clinical authority simultaneously.

## Register
brand

## Users
Colombian women, 25–55, urban professionals in Medellín and surrounding areas. They research extensively before choosing a clinic. They are sophisticated, care about credentials and results, and are willing to pay premium prices for quality. Mobile-first — most arrive via Instagram or WhatsApp referrals. They judge quality of service by the quality of the website.

## Anti-references
- Cold clinical white + teal (generic healthcare)
- Dark aggressive luxury (not us — warmth is core)
- Cheap spa pink overload
- Generic stock photo look
- Corporate navy + gold finance aesthetic

## Strategic Principles
1. Every screen must answer "why trust this doctor?" before "what do you offer?"
2. Warmth comes first, credentials second, price never mentioned
3. Social proof (real patient names, real results) over generic claims
4. Mobile experience is primary — desktop is secondary
5. WhatsApp CTA must always be one tap away
